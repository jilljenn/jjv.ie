### Books

[![TryAlgo](/static/img/tryalgo-en.jpg){height=200 align=left}](https://amzn.to/45itxtF) [![TryAlgo](/static/img/books.jpg){height=200 align=left}](https://amzn.to/45itxtF)

With [Christoph Dürr](http://www-desir.lip6.fr/~durrc/), we explain [128 algorithms](https://tryalgo.org) for preparing coding interviews & programming contests [@Durr2016].  
All [128 algorithms in Python](https://github.com/jilljenn/tryalgo/) from the book are available on GitHub, in the Python package [tryalgo](https://pypi.python.org/pypi/tryalgo/1.2.2).

- Available in English: [***Competitive Programming with Python***](https://amzn.to/45itxtF) 🇬🇧 [@Durr2020]
- In French: [***Programmation efficace*** 🇫🇷](http://tryalgo.org/)
- In Chinese: [高效算法 🇨🇳](https://book.douban.com/subject/30210075/) [@Durr2018]
- In Taiwanese: [培養與鍛鍊程式設計的邏輯腦 🇹🇼](http://www.drmaster.com.tw/Bookinfo.asp?BookID=MP11906) [@Durr2019]
